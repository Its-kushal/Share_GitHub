Prompts ->
	01. The Email Response Assistant:
		I received this email:[paste email].
		
		Help me draft a response that:
		1) Addresses each point raised
		2) Maintains a [professional/friendly/collaborative] tone
		3) Ends with a clear next step or call-to-action
		
		Keep it under 150 words.
		
	02. The Data Interpreter:
		I'm looking at data showing [describe your data/trend].
		
		Provide:
		1) Three possible explanations for this pattern
		2) Three questions I should investigate to understand this better
		3) Potential actions I could take based on this information
		
		Keep it practical, not theoratical.
		
	03. The Presentation Builder:
		I'm creating a presentation on [topic] for [audience].
		
		Create an outline for a [X]-minute presentation including:
		1) An attemtion-grabbing opening
		2) 3-5 main points with supporting details
		3) A strong closing with clear takeaways
		
		Suggest 2-3 key slides that would have high visual impact.
	
	04. The Learning Accelerator:
		Explain [concept/topic] to me.
		
		Assume I understand [related concept you know] but I'm new to this specific topic.
		
		Include:
		1) A simple analogy, and mind map.
		2) A practical example.
		3) Three key points I should remember.
		4) Use cases and applications.
		5) Pros and cons, with competitors.
		
		If there are common misconceptions, address those too.
	
	05. The Problem-solving Partner:
		I'm facing this challenge: [describe your problem].
		
		Help me think through it by:
		1) Identifying factors I might not have considered
		2) Suggesting three different approaches to address it
		3) Asking me clarifying questions that would help refine the solution
		
		Don't just give me answers - help me think better.
